Spicy


Kerala fish curry

ingredients:
  • fish:half kg.
  • kashmiri chilli powder:2tablespoon
  • chilli powder:half tablespoon
  • turmeric powder:1 pinch.
  • brindleberry:3 pieces
  • ginger:1 piece
  • garlic:6 cloves
  • shallot:6 no.s
  • curry leaves:1 sprig
  • fenugreek seeds:half teaspoon
  • mustard:half teaspoon
  • oil:2.5 tablespoon
  • salt:required
procedure:
  1. clean fish and keep aside
  2. soak brindleberry in two cup of hot water and add salt.
  3. chop shallot,ginger,garlic finely.
  5. kashmir chilli powder,chilli powder,turmeric should be made a paste by adding water
  6. heat 2 table spoon oil in a cooking pot and add mustard,fenugreek seeds.when it sputters,add finely chopped ginger,garlic and shallot
  7. stir it well until it becomes golden brown.then lower the flame and add chilli turmeric paste to it.
  8. then add soaked brindleberry and water and boil it.
  9. when it boils add fish to it,then add salt.
  10. reduce the flame and cook till it is done.after it is ready,add curry leaves to it.
  11. let the curry remain in cooking pot for some time to allow spices to seep into the fish
