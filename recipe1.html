RecipeMaster is a website which showcases the recipes of some dishes in Kerala.
The food items are divided on the basis of three preferences:healthy, quick and easy,continental.

Kerala is the land of exotic spices, so do not be surprised by the spicy curries and dishes of this state. Kerala cuisine is a blend of both vegetarian and non vegetarian dishes but spiciness and taste assuring that it lingers long in your mind. Along with the spicy curries and biriyanis there are desserts like payasam, halwa of different varieties. There is even a street in Kozhikode district named Mittayi theruvu, meaning Sweet Street with hundreds of shops selling all kinds of sweets including the famous Kozhikode halwa in various flavours and textures.
